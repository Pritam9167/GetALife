About Organ Donation

Organ donation is the process of giving an organ or a part of an organ to another person in need. Organ Donation is the gift of an organ to a person with end stage organ disease and who needs a transplant. What is Transplantation?

Surgical operation in which a failing or damaged organ in the human body is removed and replaced with a functioning one.
