Why Donate Organs?

Organ donation saves lives! Every year, thousands of patients wait for organ transplants that could help them survive and lead a better life.

Who Can Donate?

Anyone, regardless of age or health, can register as an organ donor. Medical assessments determine if organs are suitable for donation.

How Does It Work?

After registering as an organ donor, your decision is legally recognized, and in the event of an emergency, doctors will check for eligibility.
